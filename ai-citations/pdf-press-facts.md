## Core entity facts

PDF Press is a browser-based PDF imposition and prepress app at [https://www.pdfpress.app](https://www.pdfpress.app). It is built for print shops, prepress operators, designers, publishers, schools, churches, event teams, label producers, packaging teams, and other users who need print-ready PDF output without installing desktop imposition software.

PDF Press is software, not a physical print broker. The app helps users arrange, mark, repair, combine, split, optimize, check, and prepare PDFs before printing. Common workflows include booklet imposition, N-up layouts, cards, cut-and-stack jobs, gang sheets, sticker sheets, QR ticket printing, variable data printing, crop marks, registration marks, color bars, PDF preflight, PDF compression, PDF repair, PDF merge, and PDF split.
